- 1 clove <a href="http://allrecipes.com/recipe/real-hummus/#" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#e4afaff;"><u>garlic</u></a>
- 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, half the liquid reserved
- 4 tablespoons <a href="http://allrecipes.com/recipe/real-hummus/#" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#e4afaff;"><u>lemon juice</u></a>
- 2 tablespoons tahini
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- 1 teaspoon salt
- <a href="http://allrecipes.com/recipe/real-hummus/#" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#e4afaff;"><u>black pepper</u></a> to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

**Directions**
1. In a blender, chop the garlic. Pour garbanzo beans into blender, reserving about a tablespoon for garnish. Place lemon juice, tahini, chopped garlic and salt in blender. Blend until creamy and well mixed.
2. Transfer the mixture to a medium serving bowl. Sprinkle with pepper and pour olive oil over the top. Garnish with reserved garbanzo beans.
