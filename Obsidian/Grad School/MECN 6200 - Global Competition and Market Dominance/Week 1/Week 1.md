### Opportunity Cost

Opportunity Cost is the cost associated with _not_ performing a task (such as expanding to a new market)

Wiki: Opportunity cost is the loss or the benefit that could have been enjoyed if the alternative choice was chosen

### Exchange Rates

The ratio of how much a currency is worth in other currencies (1.2 EUR~1 USD). Useful to consider value of commodities in trade ( and weighing opportunity cost therein)

### Elasticity

Elasticity is measure of how sensitive the quantity demanded of it is to its price.

$MR=P(1-{1 \over n})$

Where MR is Marginal Revenue, P is Price, and _n_ is elasticity
